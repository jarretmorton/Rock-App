Set up RockID

This app runs entirely in your browser. You bring your own free Gemini API key; it's stored only on this device and sent straight to Google — never to us.

  1. Open aistudio.google.com and sign in with Google.
  2. Click Get API key → Create API key.
  3. Copy the key (starts with AIza…) and paste it below.

The honest fine print
  • Your photos go directly from your browser to Google's API.
  • On the free tier, Google may use your inputs to improve its products.
  • The free tier isn't available in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland.
  • Photos are kept only if you tap "Save to library"; saved specimens live in this browser's local storage on your device and are never uploaded to us. Your key never leaves this device except in the request header to Google.

Photograph the rock

Tips for a good photo
  • Use natural light; avoid harsh shadows.
  • Fill the frame with the rock.
  • Include a coin for scale.
  • A wet surface often shows color and grain better than dry.
